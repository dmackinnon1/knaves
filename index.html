The Island of Knights and Knaves

Knights and Knave Puzzles

On the island of Knights and Knaves, everyone is either a Knave, or a Knight. Knights always tell the truth, and Knaves always lie. You have encountered a group of islanders, and want to know who the knaves in the group are. The islanders have made some statments about each other - each statement should be taken independently: each is either a true statement or a false statement.




Next to the name of each islander, check off the ones that you think are knaves. When you think you have the correct list, click the 'solve' button to see if have solved the puzzle.
